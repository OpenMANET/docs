# How to flash OpenMANET on HaLow Link 2

---

### 1. Connect to the HaLow Link 2 Wi-Fi network

The SSID and password are on the label on the side of the device.

![Step 1 screenshot](https://images.tango.us/workflows/12a8c7b2-4d07-4de3-a1a4-212eecadd187/steps/6af42baf-3dde-43a9-9742-0b587b3f926d/1fc489d6-1a27-4d4a-af32-549bbf3894ee.png?crop=focalpoint&fit=crop&fp-x=0.9048&fp-y=0.7877&fp-z=2.0000&w=1200&border=2%2CF4F2F7&border-radius=8%2C8%2C8%2C8&border-radius-inner=8%2C8%2C8%2C8)

### 2. Click on properties

![Step 2 screenshot](https://images.tango.us/workflows/12a8c7b2-4d07-4de3-a1a4-212eecadd187/steps/3514c59e-1dd1-439d-875e-c7efdf16bd95/e2e1a75f-0efa-4023-a8a2-cdb889ce38f0.png?crop=focalpoint&fit=crop&fp-x=0.9838&fp-y=0.7252&fp-z=2.0000&w=1200&border=2%2CF4F2F7&border-radius=8%2C8%2C8%2C8&border-radius-inner=8%2C8%2C8%2C8)

### 3. Remember the gateway IP address

![Step 3 screenshot](https://images.tango.us/workflows/12a8c7b2-4d07-4de3-a1a4-212eecadd187/steps/711c9a2f-c953-4d53-9c84-30d169446aa7/7e6d994f-2b8d-46ae-ac57-520646312d28.png?crop=focalpoint&fit=crop&fp-x=0.4904&fp-y=0.6875&fp-z=2.0000&w=1200&border=2%2CF4F2F7&border-radius=8%2C8%2C8%2C8&border-radius-inner=8%2C8%2C8%2C8&mark-x=371&mark-y=414&m64=aHR0cHM6Ly9pbWFnZXMudGFuZ28udXMvc3RhdGljL2JsYW5rLnBuZz9tYXNrPWNvcm5lcnMmYm9yZGVyPTYlMkNGRjc0NDImdz0yOTcmaD0yMyZmaXQ9Y3JvcCZjb3JuZXItcmFkaXVzPTEw)

### 4. In your browser type in the gateway IP address. If you get this warning hit Advanced

![Step 4 screenshot](https://images.tango.us/workflows/12a8c7b2-4d07-4de3-a1a4-212eecadd187/steps/7cce3a6b-f7f5-4d7c-badd-4b4358341219/9210d899-14c9-4182-af42-f7316510d41e.jpg?q=100&crop=focalpoint&fit=crop&fp-x=0.5000&fp-y=0.5000&w=1200&border=2%2CF4F2F7&border-radius=8%2C8%2C8%2C8&border-radius-inner=8%2C8%2C8%2C8)

### 5. Then click Continue

![Step 5 screenshot](https://images.tango.us/workflows/12a8c7b2-4d07-4de3-a1a4-212eecadd187/steps/1b7148ca-3660-4155-8031-ef8cf501145b/38e9f870-da1c-45f0-ab33-889141b7692e.jpg?q=100&crop=focalpoint&fit=crop&fp-x=0.4062&fp-y=0.5076&fp-z=2.0000&w=1200&border=2%2CF4F2F7&border-radius=8%2C8%2C8%2C8&border-radius-inner=8%2C8%2C8%2C8)

### 6. Enter the root password

The Device Password can be found on the label on the side of the HaLow Link 2.

![Step 6 screenshot](https://images.tango.us/workflows/12a8c7b2-4d07-4de3-a1a4-212eecadd187/steps/565595ef-7b26-4932-9201-5c0c9f808fc3/cd852301-ad92-4019-8c78-c3f05936391e.jpg?q=100&crop=focalpoint&fit=crop&fp-x=0.1078&fp-y=0.2430&fp-z=2.0000&w=1200&border=2%2CF4F2F7&border-radius=8%2C8%2C8%2C8&border-radius-inner=8%2C8%2C8%2C8)

### 7. Click Login

![Step 7 screenshot](https://images.tango.us/workflows/12a8c7b2-4d07-4de3-a1a4-212eecadd187/steps/801e5a24-7cb8-44a4-89d1-66cc9ea26b28/dc6c2fc4-8ac8-4926-ae59-3ec0672d14f6.png?crop=focalpoint&fit=crop&fp-x=0.1676&fp-y=0.3580&fp-z=2.0000&w=1200&border=2%2CF4F2F7&border-radius=8%2C8%2C8%2C8&border-radius-inner=8%2C8%2C8%2C8)

### 8. Click on Advanced Config

![Step 8 screenshot](https://images.tango.us/workflows/12a8c7b2-4d07-4de3-a1a4-212eecadd187/steps/cd7ca70d-77b9-4acb-9207-855b151d0374/20d2b491-662e-4e15-9c18-2cb11892e2ca.jpg?q=100&crop=focalpoint&fit=crop&fp-x=0.0457&fp-y=0.3472&fp-z=2.0000&w=1200&border=2%2CF4F2F7&border-radius=8%2C8%2C8%2C8&border-radius-inner=8%2C8%2C8%2C8)

### 9. Click on System

![Step 9 screenshot](https://images.tango.us/workflows/12a8c7b2-4d07-4de3-a1a4-212eecadd187/steps/cce391c9-8cc7-4109-bbd9-ce0fc2a0e408/53c1402c-06f7-4b3f-90e7-ae7f821ff8c7.jpg?q=100&crop=focalpoint&fit=crop&fp-x=0.0636&fp-y=0.4472&fp-z=2.0000&w=1200&border=2%2CF4F2F7&border-radius=8%2C8%2C8%2C8&border-radius-inner=8%2C8%2C8%2C8)

### 10. Click on Backup/Flash Firmware

![Step 10 screenshot](https://images.tango.us/workflows/12a8c7b2-4d07-4de3-a1a4-212eecadd187/steps/6603b98b-b125-419c-8e70-a55dcd56a8b0/f82c7ed3-ebbe-4129-a431-3231416f140d.png?crop=focalpoint&fit=crop&fp-x=0.2503&fp-y=0.5455&fp-z=2.0000&w=1200&border=2%2CF4F2F7&border-radius=8%2C8%2C8%2C8&border-radius-inner=8%2C8%2C8%2C8)

### 11. Click on Flash Image

![Step 11 screenshot](https://images.tango.us/workflows/12a8c7b2-4d07-4de3-a1a4-212eecadd187/steps/a07ceeef-b943-4e26-9a16-93ce7d9b6241/9b508155-8132-43a2-8a64-3bc52b66e9d9.png?crop=focalpoint&fit=crop&fp-x=0.4333&fp-y=0.5859&fp-z=2.0000&w=1200&border=2%2CF4F2F7&border-radius=8%2C8%2C8%2C8&border-radius-inner=8%2C8%2C8%2C8)

### 12. Click Browse

![Step 12 screenshot](https://images.tango.us/workflows/12a8c7b2-4d07-4de3-a1a4-212eecadd187/steps/9f585c95-294e-4cd0-a28a-3a26dfb6b1e4/6991e779-8af9-42dd-a40a-8ad3452636ee.jpg?q=100&crop=focalpoint&fit=crop&fp-x=0.4128&fp-y=0.2013&fp-z=2.0000&w=1200&border=2%2CF4F2F7&border-radius=8%2C8%2C8%2C8&border-radius-inner=8%2C8%2C8%2C8)

### 13. Select the image you want to flash

The latest images can be found at [https://github.com/OpenMANET/firmware/releases](https://github.com/OpenMANET/firmware/releases). Look for the file with `halowlink2` in the filename.

![Step 13 screenshot](https://images.tango.us/workflows/12a8c7b2-4d07-4de3-a1a4-212eecadd187/steps/0d40ad60-bda8-41fc-8cf0-063e883c8038/29ddb403-1345-4ed5-99cf-83034bd75082.png?crop=focalpoint&fit=crop&fp-x=0.2518&fp-y=0.2799&fp-z=2.0000&w=1200&border=2%2CF4F2F7&border-radius=8%2C8%2C8%2C8&border-radius-inner=8%2C8%2C8%2C8)

### 14. Click Open

![Step 14 screenshot](https://images.tango.us/workflows/12a8c7b2-4d07-4de3-a1a4-212eecadd187/steps/f0878098-56d1-49cf-b43c-f5a6dd36ea14/b2af15a8-8133-4857-bf1e-89713df424ca.png?crop=focalpoint&fit=crop&fp-x=0.2781&fp-y=0.2968&fp-z=2.0000&w=1200&border=2%2CF4F2F7&border-radius=8%2C8%2C8%2C8&border-radius-inner=8%2C8%2C8%2C8)

### 15. Click Upload

![Step 15 screenshot](https://images.tango.us/workflows/12a8c7b2-4d07-4de3-a1a4-212eecadd187/steps/53ddbe10-bef4-4463-bd19-ed3fc1172122/1f376dbf-b840-49b8-9b2c-4d02b4259e3c.jpg?q=100&crop=focalpoint&fit=crop&fp-x=0.4948&fp-y=0.2500&fp-z=2.0000&w=1200&border=2%2CF4F2F7&border-radius=8%2C8%2C8%2C8&border-radius-inner=8%2C8%2C8%2C8)

### 16. Wait for the image to be verified

![Step 16 screenshot](https://images.tango.us/workflows/12a8c7b2-4d07-4de3-a1a4-212eecadd187/steps/f753a7e0-3c03-46a6-be5c-51a86f3ac416/a7556430-0ec3-4aad-8bf5-d4368f8c309a.jpg?q=100&crop=focalpoint&fit=crop&fp-x=0.4978&fp-y=0.2500&fp-z=2.0000&w=1200&border=2%2CF4F2F7&border-radius=8%2C8%2C8%2C8&border-radius-inner=8%2C8%2C8%2C8)

### 17. Uncheck "Keep settings and retain config"

![Step 17 screenshot](https://images.tango.us/workflows/12a8c7b2-4d07-4de3-a1a4-212eecadd187/steps/195cb90b-b15c-471c-8dad-f200489af998/5e03fbd4-d12f-4f44-872d-8da55034689f.png?crop=focalpoint&fit=crop&fp-x=0.4948&fp-y=0.3085&fp-z=2.0000&w=1200&border=2%2CF4F2F7&border-radius=8%2C8%2C8%2C8&border-radius-inner=8%2C8%2C8%2C8)

### 18. Scroll down and check "Force upgrade"

![Step 18 screenshot](https://images.tango.us/workflows/12a8c7b2-4d07-4de3-a1a4-212eecadd187/steps/195e3e25-4e84-4077-b89c-eafe1ca29cd7/f98617cb-ebb3-49e1-9654-b980474a985e.png?crop=focalpoint&fit=crop&fp-x=0.5278&fp-y=0.7513&fp-z=2.0000&w=1200&border=2%2CF4F2F7&border-radius=8%2C8%2C8%2C8&border-radius-inner=8%2C8%2C8%2C8)

### 19. Click Continue

This will start the flash. Wait at least 10 minutes and keep the device plugged into power.

![Step 19 screenshot](https://images.tango.us/workflows/12a8c7b2-4d07-4de3-a1a4-212eecadd187/steps/f5d22968-a22c-4978-b304-30c988cbc0fe/baaaa261-aa9c-4918-b87c-2a59d3daf351.png?crop=focalpoint&fit=crop&fp-x=0.5000&fp-y=0.5000&w=1200&border=2%2CF4F2F7&border-radius=8%2C8%2C8%2C8&border-radius-inner=8%2C8%2C8%2C8)

### 20. Connect to the openmanet Wi-Fi network

Once flashing is complete, a Wi-Fi network called **openmanet** will appear. The password is `openmanet`.

![Step 20 screenshot](https://images.tango.us/workflows/12a8c7b2-4d07-4de3-a1a4-212eecadd187/steps/49680292-4ec6-47a8-b04e-dc3ea5f8edc8/da5841cb-ddf4-4fc3-acc5-252c76871457.png?crop=focalpoint&fit=crop&fp-x=0.8111&fp-y=0.7981&fp-z=2.0000&w=1200&border=2%2CF4F2F7&border-radius=8%2C8%2C8%2C8&border-radius-inner=8%2C8%2C8%2C8)

### 21. Access the OpenMANET web interface

A freshly flashed device can be found at `10.41.254.1`.

![Step 21 screenshot](https://images.tango.us/workflows/12a8c7b2-4d07-4de3-a1a4-212eecadd187/steps/649b013f-c90c-4d06-82ef-ae24df088331/98633eac-e24f-4847-babf-dcfa90329da4.png?crop=focalpoint&fit=crop&fp-x=0.2503&fp-y=0.2500&fp-z=2.0000&w=1200&border=2%2CF4F2F7&border-radius=8%2C8%2C8%2C8&border-radius-inner=8%2C8%2C8%2C8)

### 22. Click Login

There is no password set on a fresh flash, so just click Login.

![Step 22 screenshot](https://images.tango.us/workflows/12a8c7b2-4d07-4de3-a1a4-212eecadd187/steps/4cb05cfd-65b4-4481-9e33-29eeeddb0cdb/e0e841cf-38f8-430f-852b-859953bf3b86.png?crop=focalpoint&fit=crop&fp-x=0.2503&fp-y=0.2500&fp-z=2.0000&w=1200&border=2%2CF4F2F7&border-radius=8%2C8%2C8%2C8&border-radius-inner=8%2C8%2C8%2C8)

### 23. Complete the setup wizard

Select your country and finish the device setup.

![Step 23 screenshot](https://images.tango.us/workflows/12a8c7b2-4d07-4de3-a1a4-212eecadd187/steps/866f2b3e-c03f-4442-b5a9-058ed8706f2c/b319eab4-cfa5-484a-b135-c5861e5edc43.jpg?q=100&crop=focalpoint&fit=crop&fp-x=0.4860&fp-y=0.4192&fp-z=2.0000&w=1200&border=2%2CF4F2F7&border-radius=8%2C8%2C8%2C8&border-radius-inner=8%2C8%2C8%2C8)
